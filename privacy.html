Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

HokmAI is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data or card images. All AI analysis happens on your device or is processed and immediately discarded.

1. Information We Collect

HokmAI collects minimal information necessary to provide the service:

  • Camera access: The app uses your device camera to capture photos of your cards. These images are used solely for card recognition and are not stored or transmitted to any server.
  • No account required: HokmAI does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information.
  • No location data: We do not access or collect your location.
  • No analytics: We do not use third-party analytics tools that track your behavior.

2. How We Use Information

The photos you take within HokmAI are used exclusively to:

  • Identify the cards in your hand using AI image recognition
  • Recommend the optimal trump suit based on your cards

Card images are processed locally on your device and are never saved, uploaded to external servers, or used for any other purpose.

3. Data Storage

HokmAI does not store any personal data, card images, or game history on our servers. Any temporary data used during a session is cleared when you close the app.

4. Third-Party Services

HokmAI does not share your data with third parties. We do not use advertising networks, social media plugins, or any third-party tracking services.

5. Camera Permission

HokmAI requires access to your device camera to photograph your cards. You can revoke camera access at any time through your iPhone Settings. Without camera access, the core functionality of the app will not be available.

6. Children's Privacy

HokmAI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

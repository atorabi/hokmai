What is Hokm?

Hokm is a beloved Persian trick-taking card game played with 4 players in 2 teams. One player — the Hakem — must choose a trump suit based on their first 5 cards, before seeing the rest. HokmAI analyzes those 5 cards and gives you the AI edge to make that critical decision with confidence.
